FOR THE WES VERIFICATION

Candidate who seeks for World Education Service (WES) verification / Document Verification has to follow guidelines and fill form accordingly. WES verification require for students and immigrants planning to study or work in the U.S. or Canada. Documents required at WES are (i) all semesters Mark-Sheet/Grade Card, (ii) Diploma/Degree Certificate, (iii) Transcript and (iv) latest WES Academic Records Request Form filled with WES reference no, your detail and your sign. (Apply for each degree separately)
