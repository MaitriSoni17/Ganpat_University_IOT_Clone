FAQ

1: What is Admission procedure?

Through ACPDC, Ahmedabad and it is online admission process. However, based on management quota some direct admissions are possible based on merit.

2: Is the Degree Approved by UGC 2(f)?

Ganpat University as a well reputed State Private University established in 2005 through the State Legislative act no 19 of 2005, Government of Gujarat and recognized by the UGC under the section 2(f) of the UGC Act and AICTE New Delhi.

3: How is transportation facility?

Transport facility is available from Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Mehsana, Visnagar, Vijapur, Palanpur, Unjha, Patan, Kalol etc.

4: What is the fees of the College?

Tuition Fee is 43,000* per year for Diploma Engineering.

5: What about other amenities on campus?

We have Banking facilities with ATMs, SBI Bank and Mehsana Urban Bank. Post office is also available on campus. There is a shopping Centre and canteen which serves hygienic food.

6: What are the additional benefits?

Here After completion of diploma the students will get Diploma Certificate of University. If student wants to be remain continue for degree course in same campus then he/she will get 25% concession in tuition fees of Degree course.

7: Is there any support provided for education loan?

College provides all required documents as per the need to get the education loan.

8: Can I get an admission without appearing in ACPDC counselling Process?

At the end, on vacant seats, as per circular of ACPDC guideline and procedure candidates are allowed to take admission on vacant quota.

9: What is Admission Eligibility?

Std. 10 from the Gujarat Board or Open Schooling with subjects (1) Mathematics, (2) Science (3) English or equivalent examination shall be eligible for admission.

10: Does Institute provide any other scholarship Facility?

Yes, SC, ST and OBC students sanction for Scholarship through Government.
